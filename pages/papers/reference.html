The Gallery Statement

This exhibition captures a singular moment in time—when six accomplished professionals transcended the boundaries of their individual expertise to create something greater than the sum of their parts. In the intimate setting of a Gangnam establishment, conversations became brushstrokes, experiences became canvas, and connections became art.
